Pneumonia

Lung infection causing inflammation.

  • Cough with phlegm
  • Fever and chills
  • Shortness of breath
  • Chest pain

Causes:

  • Bacterial, viral, or fungal infections
  • Weakened immune system

Precautions:

  • Get vaccinated
  • Practice good hygiene
  • Avoid smoking

Medicines:

  • Antibiotics (for bacterial pneumonia)
  • Antiviral drugs (for viral pneumonia)

  • Home Remedies:

    • Steam inhalation (relieves congestion)
    • Honey and ginger tea (soothes throat)

Food Recommendations:

  • Warm soups and broths
  • Garlic and turmeric
  • Hydrating fluids

Food to Avoid:

  • Sugary foods & drinks:weaken immunity and increase mucus production
  • Processed foods:may cause inflammation
  • Dairy (in excess):can thicken mucus in some people
  • Alcohol:suppresses immune function
