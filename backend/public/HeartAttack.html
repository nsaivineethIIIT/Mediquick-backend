Heart Attack (Myocardial Infarction)

A blockage of blood flow to the heart muscle.


Symptoms:

  • Chest pain or discomfort
  • Shortness of breath
  • Cold sweats
  • Nausea or vomiting
  • Pain in arms, neck, jaw, or back

Causes:

  • Coronary artery disease (plaque buildup)
  • High cholesterol and high blood pressure
  • Smoking, obesity, and diabetes
  • Sedentary lifestyle and excessive stress

Precautions:

  • Maintain a healthy diet and exercise regularly
  • Control blood pressure and cholesterol levels
  • Avoid smoking and excessive alcohol consumption
  • Manage stress

Medicines:

  • Aspirin (to prevent blood clots)
  • Beta-blockers (to reduce heart workload)
  • Nitroglycerin (to relieve chest pain)

  • Home Remedies:

    • Garlic (reduces cholesterol)
    • Hawthorn berry tea (improves heart function)
    • Flaxseeds and chia seeds (rich in Omega-3)

Food Recommendations:

  • Leafy greens (spinach, kale)
  • Berries (antioxidants for heart health)
  • Fatty fish (salmon, mackerel)
  • Nuts and seeds (walnuts, almonds)

Food to Avoid:

  • Trans fats: (margarine, fast food, packaged baked goods)
  • Excess salt: (processed foods, canned soups, chips) – raises blood pressure
  • Saturated fats: (butter, fatty meats, full-fat dairy) – increases cholesterol
  • Sugary drinks: lead to obesity and diabetes, increasing heart disease risk
  • Processed meats: high in sodium and preservatives
