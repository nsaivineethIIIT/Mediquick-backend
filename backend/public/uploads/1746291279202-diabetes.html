Diabetes

A metabolic disorder leading to high blood sugar.

Symptoms:

  • Frequent urination
  • Excessive thirst and hunger
  • Fatigue and blurred vision
  • Slow healing of wounds

Causes:

  • Insulin resistance (Type 2)
  • Autoimmune destruction of insulin-producing cells (Type 1)
  • Genetic and lifestyle factors

Precautions:

  • Maintain a low-carb diet
  • Exercise regularly
  • Monitor blood sugar levels
  • Avoid excessive sugar intake

Medicines:

  • Metformin (for Type 2 diabetes)
  • Insulin therapy (for Type 1 diabetes)

  • Home Remedies:

    • Bitter gourd juice (lowers blood sugar)
    • Cinnamon (improves insulin sensitivity)
    • Fenugreek seeds (help control blood sugar)

Food Recommendations:

  • Whole grains (quinoa, oats)
  • Leafy greens and non-starchy vegetables
  • Nuts and seeds (almonds, walnuts)

Food to Avoid:

  • Sugary foods: (cakes, candies, sodas) – cause blood sugar spikes
  • Refined carbs: (white bread, white rice, pasta) – rapidly increase glucose levels
  • Fried foods:increase insulin resistance
  • Full-fat dairy: contains high saturated fat
  • Processed snacks: hidden sugars and unhealthy fats
