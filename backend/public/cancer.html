Cancer (General)

A disease where cells divide uncontrollably.

Symptoms:

  • Unexplained weight loss
  • Fatigue and weakness
  • Persistent pain or lumps
  • Skin changes or abnormal bleeding

Causes:

  • Genetic mutations
  • Smoking, alcohol, and radiation exposure
  • Poor diet and lack of physical activity
  • Chronic infections (e.g., HPV, hepatitis)

Precautions:

  • Regular screenings (especially for high-risk individuals)
  • Avoid smoking and alcohol
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in antioxidants
  • Exercise regularly

Medicines:

  • Chemotherapy drugs: Doxorubicin, Cisplatin
  • Targeted therapies: Imatinib, Trastuzumab

  • Home Remedies:

    • Green tea (rich in antioxidants)
    • Turmeric (curcumin has anti-cancer properties)
    • Ginger and garlic (anti-inflammatory)

Food Recommendations:

  • Cruciferous vegetables (broccoli, cauliflower)
  • Citrus fruits (oranges, lemons)
  • Nuts and seeds (flaxseeds, walnuts)

Food to Avoid:

  • Processed meats:(hot dogs, sausages, bacon) – increases cancer risk
  • Excess red meat:(beef, pork, lamb) – linked to colorectal cancer
  • Alcohol:major risk factor for liver cancer
  • Sugary foods:may promote inflammation and tumor growth
  • Highly processed foods:(instant noodles, chips) – contain carcinogens
