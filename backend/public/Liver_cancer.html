Liver Cancer

Cancer affecting the liver cells.


Symptoms:

  • Jaundice (yellowing of skin and eyes)
  • Unexplained weight loss
  • Swollen abdomen
  • Loss of appetite

Causes:

  • Chronic hepatitis B/C infections
  • Cirrhosis due to alcohol abuse
  • Fatty liver disease
  • Exposure to aflatoxins

Precautions:

  • Get vaccinated for hepatitis B
  • Avoid excessive alcohol consumption
  • Maintain a healthy weight

Medicines:

  • Sorafenib (targeted therapy)
  • Chemotherapy drugs (Doxorubicin)

  • Home Remedies:

    • Green tea (protects liver cells)
    • Turmeric (anti-inflammatory)

Food Recommendations:

  • Leafy greens (kale, spinach)
  • Whole grains (brown rice, quinoa)
  • Omega-3 fatty acids (fish, walnuts)

Food to Avoid:

  • Processed meats:(hot dogs, sausages, bacon) – increases cancer risk
  • Excess red meat:(beef, pork, lamb) – linked to colorectal cancer
  • Alcohol:major risk factor for liver cancer
  • Sugary foods:may promote inflammation and tumor growth
  • Highly processed foods:(instant noodles, chips) – contain carcinogens
