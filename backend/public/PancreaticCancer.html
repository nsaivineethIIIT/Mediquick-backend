Pancreatic Cancer

Cancer in the pancreas.

Symptoms:

  • Abdominal pain
  • Jaundice
  • Unexplained weight loss

Causes:

  • Genetic and Hereditary Factors
  • Chronic Pancreatitis (Long-term Pancreatic Inflammation)
  • Diabetes

Precautions:

  • Avoid smoking
  • Avoid alcohol
  • Avoid processed foods

Medicines:

  • Chemotherapy drugs (Gemcitabine)
  • Immunotherapy Drugs

  • Home Remedies:

    • Ginger tea (reduces nausea)
    • Garlic: May help slow cancer progression.
    • Omega-3 Fatty Acids (Fish Oil, Flaxseeds): Reduces inflammation and supports overall health.

Food Recommendations:

  • Leafy greens and berries
  • Healthy fats (olive oil, nuts)
  • Best Foods to Eat
  • Fiber-Rich Foods

Food to Avoid:

  • Fatty, Greasy & Fried Foods (Difficult to Digest)
  • Sugary Foods & Beverages (Can Cause Blood Sugar Spikes)
  • Red & Processed Meats (Linked to Cancer Growth)
  • Alcohol & Caffeine (Can Damage the Pancreas Further)
